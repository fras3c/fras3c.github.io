About Me

I am an Assistant Professor of Computer Science (SSD ING-INF/05) at the University of Calabria, specializing in cyber security, artificial intelligence and game theory. My research focuses on developing novel methods based on AI and game-theoretic algorithms to address complex cybersecurity challenges, providing innovative and practical solutions.

Previously, I was a Research Fellow at University of Calabria. I received my Ph.D. in Computer Science in 2016 from the University of Calabria under the supervision of Prof. Francesco Scarcello and Prof. Gianluigi Greco.
